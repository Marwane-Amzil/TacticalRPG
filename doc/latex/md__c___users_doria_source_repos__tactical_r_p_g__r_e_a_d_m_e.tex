\hypertarget{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{About}\label{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}
This is a tactical RPG game made in C++ using the SFML library. The game is a turn-\/based strategy game where the player controls a team of 5 characters. The goal of the game is to defeat all enemies on the map. The player can move their characters around the map and attack enemies. The player can also use items to heal their characters or to inflict damage on enemies. The player can also use special abilities to inflict damage on enemies. The game is turn-\/based, so the player can only move and attack once per turn. The enemies also have a turn, and they will move towards the player\textquotesingle{}s characters and attack them. The game ends when all enemies are defeated or when all player characters are defeated.\hypertarget{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysection{Installation}\label{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}

The game starts with a menu. The player can choose to start the game, check the credits, edit the settings of the game or quit the game.\hypertarget{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md7}{}\doxysubsection{Game}\label{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md7}
Before starting the game, players will need to choose their characters to form their team. Each player will be using a set of 5 characters. Players will select their characters from a list of 5 characters, each being either red or blue for team blue and team red. Once the selection is done, the game will compute the \char`\"{}will\char`\"{} of each character to get the total will of your team. It will determine who\textquotesingle{}s going first. The game will then start. The player will be able to move their characters around the map and attack enemies. The player can also use items to heal their characters or to inflict damage on enemies. To do an action with a character, you have to click on a character and a menu will be shown. The player can also use special abilities to inflict damage on enemies. The game is turn-\/based, so the player can only move and attack once per turn. The enemies also have a turn, and they will move towards the player\textquotesingle{}s characters and attack them.\hypertarget{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md8}{}\doxysection{Features}\label{md__c___users_doria_source_repos__tactical_r_p_g__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md8}
